# Tales of the Verdant Wind
A Fabric mod for Minecraft that expands the wolf and player combat experience by introducing new enchantments, skills, and behavior systems.

## Overview

This mod introduces a new enchantment system for wolf armor, giving the wolves more powerful offensive damage output and passive abilities in combat. Wolves can inflict debuff and other status effects on enemies, trigger skill-based abilities with cooldowns, and a few slight adjustments to the wolf and player interactions.

Players also gain buffs when they meet certain conditions, such as additional damage points, temporary immunity, status effects from items when you a specific enchantment, and more.

## Dependencies
This mod requires [Terrablender](https://modrinth.com/mod/terrablender) to work.
